Key Takeaways for BESS Investors

Extraordinary 2025 Revenues

Post-BRELL scarcity: aFRR Up EUR 29.56/MW mean per 15-min (vs EUR 0.87 regulated in 2024). Baltic FCR EUR 145/MW/h on launch. Spikes to EUR 9,976/MWh.

Revenue Compression Ahead

454 MW installed → 2,000+ MW by 2030. "Opportunity won't last forever." Early movers capture 3-5x returns vs late entrants.

Duration Matters

4h BESS earns ~3.3x DA arbitrage of 1h. But aFRR/FCR capacity premium only ~1.4x. Optimal depends on market mix and timing.

Structural Drivers Persist

Baltic "energy island." Estlink 2 failures → 65-71% surges. 1.7 GW renewables added 2025. Reserve fees EUR 3.70/MWh create permanent pool.

Structural Tailwinds

Growing Renewables

Lithuania targeting 5.1 GW solar, doubled wind by 2030. 1.7 GW added 2025. More renewables = more volatility = more BESS opportunity.

Interconnection Risks

Estlink 2 failure → 65-71% price surge. Baltics remain "energy island." Outages create extreme prices (EUR 9,976/MWh).

Baltic Market Integration

Joint Baltic balancing procurement launched Feb 2025. Lithuanian BESS can serve LV/EE reserves. Combined aFRR need ~600-800 MW.

Reserve Capacity Fees

EUR 3.70/MWh reserve fee (Estonia) creates permanent revenue pool for flexibility providers, independent of spot prices.

Methodology & Assumptions
  • DA Arbitrage: Perfect-foresight upper bound (buy N cheapest, sell N most expensive hours/day) x 85% capture.
  • RT efficiency: 88% (Li-ion NMC/LFP).
  • aFRR: ENTSO-E contracted reserve prices (A47). Prices per MW per 15-min ISP. BESS alternates Up/Down (50/50) for SoC.
  • aFRR availability: 1h: 65%, 2h: 80%, 4h: 90%.
  • FCR: Estimated from Baltic market data (launched Feb 2025). EUR 30/MW/h avg (first-day: EUR 145/MW/h).
  • mFRR: ENTSO-E process type A51. Lower volumes, sporadic.
  • Imbalance: |Imbalance - DA| spread. Data through Sep 2024.
  • Multi-Market Combined: aFRR 40%, FCR 20%, DA 25%, mFRR 5%, Imbalance 10%.
  • Projections: Revenue compression as BESS grows 454 MW → 2,000+ MW. Balancing compresses faster than DA.
  • All figures gross revenue before opex (~EUR 5-8/kW/yr), degradation (2-3%/yr), financing.
